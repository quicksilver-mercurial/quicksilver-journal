About the author

Hello. I am Mercurial, the author of this blog. I am not to be confused with the version control system of the same name. I chose the name Mercurial because it reflects my ever changing sense of self.

I think in general what the universe wants most from me is for me to love and appreciate it as much as possible. I believe this because the more things I love about the world, the more I enjoy being alive. Some things I enjoy in particular are science fiction, software development, politics, history, music, tabletop roleplaying games. Most of all I enjoy having nice moments with my friends and family.

I try be good, by which I mean I try to behave in a way that helps others feel better while also taking care of myself. Being good is surprisingly non-trivial, takes constant practice and effort, and I view the neverending effort to try to be good as the chief thing that I am doing with my life. I find that my ability to be good ebbs and flows with my moods and life circumstances. It is easier to be good when I feel good. Paradoxically, it is easier t o feel good when I believe I have been good latley.

I have a constantly shifting gender identity and accept any pronouns, though I tend to think of myself as a somewhat feminine person - feel free to refer to me however you'd like based on context. I do not take my gender identity very seriously.

Thank you for reading. Feel free to contact me for any reason at zakaden@gmail.com
